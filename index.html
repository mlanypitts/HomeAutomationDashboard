Energy Management Tips and Advices

Lights Out
Lighting accounts for 5% - 10% of total energy consumption which increases with the number of home users. The potential for saving energy and cost is significant. Use the Home Automation System to configure an All Lights Out automation task whenever you leave the house for work or go to sleep!
Use Appliances in Eco-Use
Achieve enormous energy and cost savings by operating energy hungry electrical appliances such as heaters in Eco-Use. Use the "Automate" feature to preheat your home at warm temperature one hour before leaving your workplace so that you don't have to operate your heater at full temperature in the evening.
Low Utility fares
Make use of your "Automate" feature to program tasks such as washing your clothes during low-utility fare hours of the day even if you are not at home or you are sleeping! Low Utility time: 10:00pm - 05:00am and 11:00am - 03:00pm.
